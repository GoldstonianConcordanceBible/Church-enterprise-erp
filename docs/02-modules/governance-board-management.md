# Governance & Board Management Module

## Purpose

Manages church governance, board activity, elder meetings, resolutions, policies, and institutional decisions.

## Core Features

- Board member profiles
- Meeting agendas
- Meeting minutes
- Resolution tracking
- Voting records
- Policy library
- Conflict of interest disclosures
- Board packets
- Committee management
- Annual meeting records
- Strategic plan tracking
- Decision archive

## Workflow

1. Meeting is scheduled
2. Agenda is created
3. Documents are attached
4. Board votes or decisions are recorded
5. Minutes are approved
6. Resolutions are archived
7. Action items are assigned
