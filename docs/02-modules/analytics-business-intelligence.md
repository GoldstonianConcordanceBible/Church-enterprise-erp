Provides dashboards, reporting, forecasting, and operational intelligence across the entire ERP.

- Volunteer engagement
- Ministry growth
- Event participation
- Payroll reporting
- Grant spending
- Donor retention
- Discipleship progression
- Facility utilization

- Media engagement
- Financial forecasting

- KPI alerts
