# Small Groups Module

## Purpose

Manages Bible studies, life groups, discipleship groups, home groups, and ministry circles.

## Core Features

- Group directory
- Group leader profiles
- Meeting schedules
- Attendance tracking
- Group capacity
- Member matching
- Curriculum assignment
- Prayer requests
- Childcare notes
- Leader reports
- Group health scoring
- Multiplication planning

## Workflow

1. Group is created
2. Leader is assigned
3. Meeting schedule is published
4. Members join or are assigned
5. Attendance and care notes are tracked
6. Leadership monitors health and growth
