# Missions & Outreach Module

## Purpose

Manages local outreach, global missions, mission trips, service projects, aid distribution, and evangelism initiatives.

## Core Features

- Mission trip planning
- Outreach project management
- Team rosters
- Participant applications
- Fundraising goals
- Sponsorship tracking
- Travel documents
- Emergency contacts
- Packing lists
- Training requirements
- Outreach attendance
- Impact reports
- Partner organizations

## Use Cases

- Food pantry
- Prison ministry
- Homeless outreach
- School supply drives
- Mission trips
- Disaster response
- Community evangelism
- International partnerships
