# Donor Development Module

## Purpose

Builds deeper relationships with donors, supporters, sponsors, and major givers.

## Core Features

- Donor profiles
- Giving history
- Donor segmentation
- Major donor pipeline
- Pledge tracking
- Campaign tracking
- Thank-you workflows
- Stewardship reports
- Donor notes
- Recurring gift analysis
- Donor retention scoring
- Sponsor management
- Restricted gift tracking

## Donor Stages

1. First-time giver
2. Repeat giver
3. Recurring giver
4. Campaign supporter
5. Major donor
6. Legacy donor
7. Foundation/Corporate sponsor
