# Discipleship Pathways Module

## Purpose

Tracks spiritual growth, formation, learning, mentorship, and leadership development.

## Core Features

- Discipleship tracks
- New believer pathway
- Baptism preparation
- Membership class
- Bible study plans
- Mentorship assignments
- Leadership development
- Course completion
- Spiritual milestones
- Prayer and care follow-up
- Certificates
- Progress dashboards

## Example Pathways

- Visitor to Member
- Member to Volunteer
- Volunteer to Leader
- New Believer to Baptism
- Small Group Member to Group Leader
- Minister in Training
