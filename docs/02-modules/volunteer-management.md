# Volunteer Management Module

## Purpose

Manages church volunteer recruitment, onboarding, scheduling, training, compliance, and retention.

## Core Features

- Volunteer profiles
- Skills and gifts database
- Availability calendar
- Ministry team assignments
- Service rotation scheduling
- Background check status
- Training completion
- Certification tracking
- Volunteer hours
- Automated reminders
- Substitute requests
- Burnout risk flags
- Team leader dashboards

## Workflow

1. Person expresses interest in serving
2. Staff reviews profile and ministry fit
3. Background check is requested if needed
4. Training path is assigned
5. Volunteer is scheduled
6. Attendance and performance are tracked
7. Retention and engagement are monitored
