# Purchasing Module

## Purpose

The Purchasing Module manages purchase requests, approvals, vendors, purchase orders, contracts, and budget controls.

## Core Features

- Purchase requests
- Purchase approvals
- Purchase orders
- Vendor management
- Vendor bidding
- Preferred vendor lists
- Contract tracking
- Department budget validation
- Ministry budget validation
- Invoice matching
- Receiving workflows
- Inventory linkage
- Approval thresholds
- Audit history

## User Roles

- Ministry Leader
- Department Manager
- Finance Officer
- Purchasing Manager
- Vendor
- Executive Pastor
- Administrator

## Workflow

1. Ministry submits purchase request
2. System checks available budget
3. Approval route is triggered
4. Purchasing reviews vendor options
5. Purchase order is created
6. Goods or services are received
7. Invoice is matched
8. Payment is routed to accounts payable
9. Ledger is updated

## Enterprise Features

- Multi-campus purchasing
- Centralized procurement
- Vendor performance scoring
- Recurring purchase templates
- Spending policy enforcement
- AI duplicate purchase detection
